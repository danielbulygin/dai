# Research Skill

When researching a topic:

1. **Clarify the question** - What exactly needs to be answered?
2. **Search broadly** - Use multiple queries and sources
3. **Evaluate sources** - Prioritize official docs, recent content, expert opinions
4. **Synthesize** - Combine findings into a coherent answer
5. **Cite sources** - Always link to where information came from
6. **State limitations** - What couldn't you find? What's uncertain?

## Output Format
- **Summary**: 2-3 sentence answer
- **Key Findings**: Bullet points
- **Sources**: Links with brief descriptions
- **Confidence**: High / Medium / Low

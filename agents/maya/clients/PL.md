# Press London — Creative Strategy Context

## Brand Overview

Cold-pressed juice and wellness brand based in London. Premium health and nutrition products with a clean, modern aesthetic.

## Products

| Product | Creative Focus |
|---------|---------------|
| **Cold-pressed juices** | Premium ingredients, health benefits, taste |
| **Juice cleanses** | Detox, reset, transformation stories |
| **Wellness shots** | Quick daily health ritual, immunity |

## Brand Voice & Visual Identity

- **Tone:** Clean, energetic, aspirational health. Not preachy — approachable wellness
- **Visual:** Bright, colorful product shots. Fresh ingredients. Urban lifestyle
- **Style:** Modern, minimal design with bold product colors as hero

### Avoid
- Medical claims or pseudo-science
- Extreme detox messaging
- Overly produced studio content — keep it fresh and relatable

## Target Customer

- **Age:** 25-40, urban professionals
- **Mindset:** Health-conscious but not extreme. Wants easy, premium health choices
- **Use case:** Morning routine, post-workout, office desk wellness

## Creative Direction

### Proven Coordinates
- **F01 × A11** (Talking Head + Transformation): "My morning routine changed" stories
- **F03 × A09** (Product Demo + Ingredient/Science): Ingredient spotlight, cold-press process
- **F16 × A10** (No-Ads Ad + Use-Case/Occasion): "POV: your morning routine" organic-style

### Underexplored
- **F13 × A02** (Compilation + Social Proof): Customer transformation mashups
- **F05 × A07** (VO + B-roll + Lifestyle): Premium brand film for awareness
- **F14 × A12** (Static + Scarcity/FOMO): Seasonal flavors, limited drops

## Key Learnings

- Health benefit hooks need specificity: "3 extra hours of energy" beats "feel amazing"
- Ingredient-forward content performs better than lifestyle-first for MOF
- Color and visual vibrancy in thumbnails drives higher CTR for static ads

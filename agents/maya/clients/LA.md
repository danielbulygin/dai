# Laori — Creative Strategy Context

## Brand Overview

Premium non-alcoholic spirits (alkoholfreie Spirituosen) for the DACH market. Positioned as an elevated lifestyle choice — not a compromise, but a deliberate decision. Italian-inspired aesthetic.

## Products

| Product | Creative Focus |
|---------|---------------|
| **Juniper No 1** | Gin alternative — botanical, refreshing, sophisticated |
| **Spice No 2** | Rum/whisky alternative — warm, complex, evening drink |
| **Ruby No 3** | Aperitif alternative — fruity, social, aperitivo occasion |

## Brand Voice & Visual Identity

- **Archetype:** Explorer — confident, witty, elevated but accessible
- **Tone:** Sophisticated without being pretentious. "I choose this" not "I can't have that"
- **Visual:** Editorial, Italian summery, premium lifestyle photography
- **Hero shot:** Drink centered, flowery CTA card, warm lighting
- **Color palette:** Warm golds, botanicals, Mediterranean tones

### Avoid
- Dark greens, all caps
- Preachy or moralistic tone about not drinking
- Positioning as a "replacement" — it's a choice, not a compromise
- Medical/health-benefit messaging (not a wellness brand)

## Target Customer

- **Age:** 28-45, urban professionals
- **Mindset:** Health-conscious, curious, socially active. Wants to enjoy social occasions without alcohol
- **Occasion-driven:** Dinner parties, aperitivo hour, after-work drinks, date nights

## Creative Direction

### Proven Coordinates
- **F05 × A07** (VO + B-roll + Lifestyle): Premium brand feel, occasion-based
- **F01 × A06** (Talking Head + Education): "Why I stopped drinking" / cocktail tutorials
- **F03 × A09** (Product Demo + Ingredient/Science): Botanical showcase, mixing demos

### Underexplored
- **F10 × A07** (ASMR/Sensory + Lifestyle): Pouring sounds, ice, botanicals — sensory appeal
- **F02 × A04** (Interview + Founder Story): Authenticity play
- **F14 × A10** (Static + Use-Case/Occasion): Occasion-specific carousel ads

## Key Learnings

- Problem-agitation hooks ("tired of sugary mocktails") outperform curiosity hooks for Laori
- Occasion-based messaging (aperitivo, dinner party) drives higher intent than generic lifestyle
- Morning-after angles are NOT effective — customers don't identify with hangover messaging
- Recipe-style content (how to make a Laori spritz) performs well as education-first TOF

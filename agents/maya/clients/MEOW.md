# Strayz (MEOW) — Creative Strategy Context

## Brand Overview

Organic cat food brand from Berlin. Activist positioning — raw, honest, anti-corporate. Radical transparency about ingredients and sourcing. Uses stray cat rescue mission as brand purpose.

## Products

| Product | Creative Focus |
|---------|---------------|
| **Wet food pouches** | Premium organic ingredients, cat welfare |
| **Dry food** | Convenience + quality positioning |

## Brand Voice & Visual Identity

- **Archetype:** Outlaw + Caregiver — raw, eye-level, "trashy fun", radically honest
- **Tone:** Anti-corporate. Talks like a real person, not a brand. Berlin energy.
- **Visual:** Behind-the-scenes, real cats, Berlin chaos, cat POV shots, imperfect footage
- **Style:** Exclusively lo-fi. Professional production kills the brand.

### Avoid
- Polished studio content (brand-killing)
- Corporate/jargon language
- Greenwashing vibes — be real, not performative
- Standard "cute cat" content without substance

## Target Customer

- **Age:** 25-40, urban cat owners
- **Mindset:** Cares about what they feed their cat. Values transparency, sustainability, honesty
- **Trigger:** Discovery that mainstream cat food is garbage → search for better options

## Creative Direction

### Proven Coordinates
- **F16 × A13** (No-Ads Ad + Myth-Busting): "What's REALLY in your cat food" — organic scroll-stop
- **F01 × A08** (Talking Head + Behind the Scenes): Founder/team talking about sourcing, rescue missions
- **F09 × A13** (Reaction/Duet + Myth-Busting): Reacting to mainstream cat food ingredients
- **F01 × A01** (Talking Head + Problem→Solution): "My cat was sick, then I switched"

### Underexplored
- **F17 × A08** (Two-Person + Behind the Scenes): Visit to a farm/supplier — transparency
- **F07 × A06** (Screen Recording + Education): Reading ingredient labels, comparing brands
- **F04 × A15** (Unboxing + First Impression): Cat's first reaction to Strayz

## Key Learnings

- Authenticity dial must be at max (0.9-1.0) — this brand lives or dies on feeling real
- Myth-busting hooks are the #1 performer: controversy drives engagement
- Cat reaction footage is gold — real cats, imperfect shots, genuine moments
- German-language content only — DACH market, German voice essential
